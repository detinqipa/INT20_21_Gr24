Top 10 zoos to visit in 2021

For those who don’t have the budget or the time to go on safari, a trip to the zoo is the next best thing when it comes to getting your wildlife fix. Though there are some zoos where welfare standards fall short, many have a strong conservation focus. Here are our picks for the 10 zoos to visit in 2021.

  1. Wellington Zoo (New Zeland)


    2. Wellington Zoo, founded in 1906, houses hundreds of animals, with different species. It occupies about 32 acres and offers its visitors amazing experiences. By visiting the Wellington Zoo, you’ll be able to see several endangered species, including the miniature sun bear and Sumatran tigers. Apart from that, you’ll also be able to learn about the lifestyles of animals like lemurs, lions, and red pandas.

  2. Moscow Zoo (Russia)


    3. Apart from being one of the best zoos in the world in 2021, Moscow Zoo is also one of the oldest zoos in Europe. By visiting the zoological park, you’ll get the opportunity to see many animals of different species. Currently, the Moscow Zoo houses about 8,300 animals and occupies a total land area of 21.5 hectares.

  3. Rotterdam Zoo (Netherlands)


    4. Rotterdam Zoo, founded in 1857, is one of the best zoos in the world in 2021. According to the dutch, you can also refer to the zoological park as Diergaarde Blijdorp. Rotterdam Zoo is divided into several regions, with each of them housing thousands of animals of over 180 different species. Also, talking about its land area, it occupies about 26 hectares.

  4. Welsh Mountain Zoo (North Wales)


    5. Welsh Mountain Zoo, established in 1963, is one of the best zoos in the world 2021. It houses several animals of different species, including birds, mammals, reptiles, amphibians, and a few others. Also, it’s located in Conwy Country and occupies a total land area of 37 acres. By visiting the zoo, you’ll get to see several beautiful animals, including California sea lions, penguins, and otters.

  5. Toronto Zoo (Canada)


    6. With over 5,000 animals of 460 different species, the Toronto Zoo is one of the best zoos in the world that you should consider visiting. It offers its visitors great experiences, including camel rides. By visiting the place, you’ll get to understand the lifestyle of many animals, including spider monkeys and antelopes. Toronto Zoo has a land area of 287 hectares and generates up to 1.5 million visitors yearly.

  6. Berlin Zoological Garden (Germany)


    7. Berlin Zoological Garden, located in Germany, is also one of the best zoos in the world that you can consider visiting for fun in 2021. It houses around 18,662 animals of different species. Also, It’s one of the oldest zoos in Europe, occupying about 86 acres of land in Berlin. However, in case you’re planning to visit the place, try and go with your own food.

  7. St. Louis Zoo (USA)


    8. St. Louis Zoo, opened in 1910, is one of the best zoological parks in the world, which you can consider visiting in 2021. It has a total of 17,000 animals of different species, including rare and endangered ones. Apart from the animals, St. Louis Zoo features animal exhibits, attractions, dining spaces, and shopping spaces.

  8. London Zoo (United Kingdom)


    9. The London Zoo is by far the oldest Scientific Zoo in the world. It started operation in London on 27 April 1828, with the intention of using it for scientific studies. However, after three years, animals from the Tower of London were moved to the collection. Talking about its land area, the London Zoo, located at Regent’s Park in London, occupies a total of 36 acres. It’s one of the best zoos in the world, which you can visit in 2021, because it has about 20,000 animals, with 673 different species.

  9. National Zoological Gardens Of Africa (South Africa)


    10. When it comes to rich wildlife, South Africa is one of the countries in Africa that you can count on. The National Zoological Gardens, informally called the Pretoria Zoo, was founded in 1899 by J. W. B. Gunning. Many people call it the Pretoria Zoo because it’s located in Pretoria, South Africa. Talking about the land area of the zoological park, it occupies a total of 85 hectares. The Pretoria Zoo is on our list of best zoos in the world in 2021 because it has up to 9,000 animals of about 700 different species.

  10. San Diego Zoo (USA)


    11. San Diego Zoo, founded in 1916, is one of the oldest zoos in the world. In 2021, San Diego Zoo, based in Balboa Park, California, is still considered one of the best zoos in the world. That’s because it has more than 3,500 animals of about 650 different species. Apart from that, the zoo also has an open-air exhibit for each of the animals. Talking about the land area of the zoo, it occupies a total of 99 acres. So, by visiting the beautiful park with your family, you’ll get to learn a lot about nature, especially the lifestyle of some of the animals.

10 Spectacular Glowing Animals

The ability to produce and emit light in the dark biochemically is called bioluminescence, and it is one of the most fascinating phenomena in the nature. While it is beautiful to look at for an observer of glow-in-the-dark creatures, bioluminescence is actually used by many creatures as part of their survival, defence and preying strategies as they use this natural ‘cold’ light to attract their prey or mates or distract predators. Humans, too, have enlarged the list with some unlikely creatures, using the advancement of science.

Who are we ?!


A group of students of the technical faculty, who set themselves the goal of realizing a website which will be valuable and useful for our society !

The idea of ​​creating such a website came from the reality in which we live. As we all know, in Kosovo, animals are not adequately protected, moreover, they are often mistreated. This website was created for educational purposes, as a project in the subject INTERNET. Although we started this project on a small scale, we aim that in the future we will turn it into a comprehensive platform which not only informs people about animals, but also plays an important role in ensuring that animals have their rights,and the laws for their protection should not remain only on paper, but should be strictly implemented. With small and safe steps, we will work until we see no stray animals in the streets. Our main purpose is to make sure that everyone knows the values of the sweetest creatures on the world and treates them the right way. We hope this platform pushes you to join our journey !!
